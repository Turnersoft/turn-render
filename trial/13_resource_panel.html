Theorem

Lagrange's Theorem

In any finite group, the order of every subgroup divides the order of the group.

If H ≤ G and |G| < ∞, then |H| divides |G|

Theorem

First Isomorphism Theorem

Every group homomorphism can be factored through its image and kernel.

If φ: G → H is a homomorphism, then G/ker(φ) ≅ Im(φ)

Theorem

Orbit-Stabilizer Theorem

For a group action, the size of an orbit times the size of its stabilizer equals the group order.

|Orbit(x)| × |Stab(x)| = |G|
